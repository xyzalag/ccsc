10th Cracow Cognitive Science Conference

12-13 May 2018, Jagiellonian University, Kraków, Poland
TECHNOLOGY

THE CONFERENCE STARTS IN...

<

We are pleased to invite you to 10th - anniversary - edition of Cracow Cognitive Science Conference - an annual event organised by Jagiellonan University's Cognitive Science Student Association and the Neurocognitive Section of Jagiellonian University's Psychology Student Association.

The conference's topic is TECHNOLOGY. The conference will be organized in two complementary modules including the specific problem areas enumerated below:

    THE FIRST MODULE - PROBLEMS
  • MACHINE ETHICS AND MACHINE LAW
  • TRANSHUMANISM
  • HUMAN-COMPUTER INTERACTION
  • GENERAL ARIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE
    THE SECOND MODULE - METHODS
  • VIRTUAL REALITY & AUGMENTED REALITY
  • NEUROIMAGING
  • SENSORY SUBSTITUTION
  • MACHINE LEARNING
